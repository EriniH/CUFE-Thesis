You can add your publications in the bibliography file (\texttt{.bib}) with the keyword \texttt{mypub} and they will be included in the publications list.

\begin{refsection}
\nocite{*}
\printbibliography[env=mypub, heading=none, resetnumbers=true, keyword=mypub]
\end{refsection}
